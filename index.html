My Story

Background

Since I was young, I have had the privilege to travel with my parents. From following my mother on her business trips to Oregon, visiting my grandmother in New Jersey, to meeting extended family in South Korea, I have grown to love traveling and experiencing new cultures. Photography was actually my father’s favorite hobby. He still has film cameras, polaroids, and a bunch of photography accessories stored somewhere in the basement, but because of his interests, it also became one of my favorite hobbies. There were times when I would be snapping photos left and right, and then I would grow tired of it and put down the camera. However, with new technology and better quality cameras on smartphones, I found it so much easier to take pictures or record videos which I can share later on my instagram or vlogs! As I am growing older, I realized that I needed to learn how to feed myself..unfortunately. So, I started watching youtube videos, and searching up recipes online for dishes that I was craving. It started off with making simple marinara spaghetti, to kimchi fried rice, and now I can make steamed buns, potstickers, and so much more! If I am not sleeping or studying, I am usually in the kitchen cooking up something new!

My Goal

Purpose

I hope that my blog can inspire and motivate you all to try new things whether it is trying a new dish, flying across the globe, or finding a new hobby! I’ll be blogging about my travel experiences, basic tips, favorite recipes and dishes, lifestyle and hopefully, I can capture all that through photos as well.
